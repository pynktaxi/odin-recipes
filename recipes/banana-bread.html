Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread can be made in one bowl, for minimal cleanup. Tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 3/4 c. of sugar
    • I added 1/2 c. of white sugar
    • and 1/4 c. of molasses
    • you can arrange the sugar however you choose as long as it's 3/4 c.
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 c. flour
  • *optional* chocolate chips
