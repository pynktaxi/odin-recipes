Southwestern Salad

Description

This Southwestern Salad can be meal prepped for a handful of meals for the week, about 5 servings. Tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • seasoning for chicken, i.e. salt and pepper

  • ranch
  • taco seasoning

  • can of corn
  • can of black beans
  • diced tomato
  • diced onion
  • lettuce, cut to bite size

Steps

  1. prepare chicken to cook in skillet, with preferred seasonings
  2. cook chicken in skillet until done then set aside
  3. while cooking, prepare vegetables
    • wash what needs to be washed
    • drain cans
    • cut what needs to be cut
    • combine vegetables in large bowl
  4. prepare dressing by mixing about 1/4 - 1/2 c. of ranch dressing with about 1 packet (or 2T. if made at home) of taco seasoning.
  5. cut chicken into bite sized pieces and combine with vegetables. Toss it all together.
  6. Add dressing to taste and enjoy :D
