Muffins

Description

These muffins are made up. I don't know a recipe, so please don't try baking this. The science will be bad.

I warned you.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 c. flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 stick of butter, melted
  • 3/4 c. of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2 c. buttermilk
  • *Mix ins* 1 c. of chocolate chips or berries of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F and grease muffin pans
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in large bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients in a smaller bowl
  4. Add the wet to dry
  5. Mixing as little as possible, combine all ingredients into batter, adding mix ins before it's completed
    • Less mixing results in less glucose production in the batter, leaving the result to be lighter textured muffins
  6. Pour batter into muffin pans, cups optional
  7. Bake at 350 for 45-55 minutes
    • It is done when a toothpick can be inserted and removed cleanly from center
  8. Serve warm with butter and enjoy :D
